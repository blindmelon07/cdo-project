About GSAC

Learn about our history, mission, and the people behind the cooperative.

Who We Are

The Gubat Saint Anthony Cooperative (GSAC) is a duly registered Credit and Savings Cooperative serving the community of Gubat, Sorsogon, Philippines. Founded on the principles of cooperation, solidarity, and mutual aid, GSAC has been providing accessible financial services to its members for over two decades. We are committed to improving the economic well-being of our members and their families through responsible credit, savings mobilization, and continuous education.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and reliable financial services to our members while promoting cooperative values of self-help, solidarity, and social responsibility within the Gubat community.

🌟 Our Vision

To be the leading cooperative in Sorsogon — financially strong, member-centered, and a pillar of sustainable community development guided by cooperative principles.

📜 Our History

GSAC was established to address the financial needs of the Gubat community. Over the years, we have grown from a small cooperative to a trusted institution, continuously expanding our services and membership base.
