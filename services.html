Our Services

Financial products designed to help members save, grow, and thrive.

💰

Savings Account

Build your financial future with GSAC's savings accounts. We offer competitive interest rates and flexible terms to help every member grow their savings.

  • Minimum deposit of ₱200
  • Earn interest on your deposits
  • No maintaining balance penalty
  • Withdrawable anytime
  • Safe and insured deposits
📊

Share Capital

As a member-owner, you contribute to the cooperative's capital. Share capital entitles you to voting rights and a share in the cooperative's dividends and patronage refunds.

  • Minimum of ₱500 share capital
  • Receive annual dividends
  • One member, one vote principle
  • Non-withdrawable (transfer only)
  • Grows as cooperative grows
🏦

Loan Products

GSAC offers various loan products to meet the financial needs of our members — from personal to livelihood and emergency purposes.

Loan Type Amount Interest Term
Regular Loan Up to ₱50,000 2% / month Up to 12 months
Emergency Loan Up to ₱10,000 1.5% / month Up to 6 months
Livelihood Loan Up to ₱30,000 1.75% / month Up to 18 months
Educational Loan Up to ₱20,000 1.5% / month Up to 12 months
🎁

Member Benefits

Being a GSAC member comes with exclusive benefits that support you and your family throughout the year.

  • Annual dividend earnings
  • Patronage refund
  • Death aid benefit
  • Loan insurance coverage
  • Access to cooperative seminars and trainings
  • Voting rights in general assemblies
